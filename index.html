ABOUT HACK WITH LETSUPGRADE

Hack with LetsUpgrade is a 72 hours-long program conducted by LetsUpgrade. Aim to help students and employees to get exposure with real time projects. They can pair each other as a group, to work on real-world software development projects and techniques in this hackathon. This organization aims to help the new developers to get best opportunities like jobs and internships, by contributing their projects to this hackathon and top winners will be surprised with new goodies and cash prizes.

"The main mantra is to technically inspire all the beginners"

ABOUT LETSUPGRADE

LetsUpgrade is an open community-centric education believing that the upskilling of a person happens in offline learning sessions with a project-based learning approach, where a student is a part of a big community and a trainer is there to help them solve problems in the league of achieving excellence in tech careers of future. At LetsUpgrade from the start, we help you upgrade yourself with industry-developed programs, on one mentorship sessions, and job assistance too.

    If you love to code, you are more than welcome to participate in the Hackathon. As this is a Hack-a-thon for developing {what we want them to develop here like Android-based applications, a background in Android programming will be helpful. Lets the Senior decide it.

  • How do I submit what I have made for the Hackathon?

    You have to develop the application on your local system and submit it on LetsUpgrade in tar/zip file format along with instructions to run the application and source code in the form that will be provided to you.

  • How is the environment?

    Will your environment support any language? Will you provide any IDE and DB for us to work on ideas? You have to develop the entire software application on your local system and submit it on HackerEarth in tar/zip file format along with instructions to run the application and source code.

  • Since there is no specific technology mentioned, are there any restrictions on using a number of pre-built libraries?

    There is no restriction to use any language, technology stack, or libraries. You can use any of them to create a web/mobile application.

  • If it is a team submission, does that mean all team members will have access to work at the same time?

    Yes, all team members can log in from their accounts and do application submission on HackerEarth.

  • Who will own the IP(Intellectual Property) Rights to the product that I have built?

    All the IP of the application will remain with Avaya, please make sure that the idea submitted is an original idea and has not been used before.

  • Do all team members have to register for the Hackathon?

    All members of a team will need to register independently on the HackerEarth portal.

  • Will I or the teams have to bear the Travel and Accommodation expenses for traveling to Pune for the event days?

    Avaya will provide the Travel and Accommodation expenses for the shortlisted participants including to/fro Air/Train fare and accommodation.
